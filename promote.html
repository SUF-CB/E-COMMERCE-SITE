Sitename

When it comes to deciding a name of an E-commerce page customers would easily require a remember able name. Therefore, the web designer must choose a domain name which is short and easy to remember compared to having a longer domain name which can be forgotten within a matter of time and days. Although some long domains can be suitable as long as they are catchy.

Suitable search engines

Choosing and maintaining an effective search engine is very attainable. When customers inputs a search on search such as Google it will output the results in chronological order from the top E-commerce’s to the lowest E-commerce’s. For this process to be carried out there are spiders which are used to move over the web and find webpages in order to give the user the top E-commerce sites. Once these fetches have been done, metatags and HTML tags are combined together which provides the content on the E-commerce page. Both tags are code and therefore, they are hidden from every user. In order for E-commerce’s to promote their pages they can contribute a payment to the search engine to promote their website on sponsored URL which will lead to customers looking at their webpage as reliable and top contender of search.

Pop Ups/Banners

New E-commerce’s can use banners onto trending websites such as social media sites like Instagram where they can advertise their E-commerce page which will get views through Instagram users as the go through their pages. Pop ups can also be attained onto a site which is users are already attaining.

Forums/Newsgroups

New E-commerce’s can use forums to socialize with customers on the web and create a discussion about the products which the organization sells and the price which customers would like to see be sold for. Newsgroups can also be attained by using messages which can be sent so online users to try and convince them to review and purchase products from the site which will attract a wide range of customers.

Customer loyalty

There are customers who prefer not to use E-commerce sites and would rather shop at their preferred store. Therefore, the organization would need slight convincing to persuade customer to shop online. This can be done in multiple ways by personalizing their site in a sufficient manner where customers would review the webpage as a reliable source. So when it comes to the customer reviewing the products they can a clear brief description of their products and they can use pictures of what is being purchased and also see high security on their E-commerce site.
