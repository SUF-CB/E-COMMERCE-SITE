What is E-commerce?

E-commerce is the electronic way of buying and selling products through the internet and it is also the transfer of money to carry out the transactions in three ways which are carried out from consumer to consumer, business to consumer and business to business.

Examples of E-Commerce are: Ebay,ASOS,Debehams,Argos and Amazon etc.
