Structure

Interfaces

Interfaces must be easy to use for different users such as older people and young children so they can browse for products easily and get them through the other stages such as payments.

Display of Goods

Display of goods will be displayed and viewed on different pages which are linked to the homepage. Therefore, when you browse for different clothing more pages will be opened so you can look for more products and goods through inputting the item required in the search bar.

Images

Images on the E-commerce site the website must be visually appealing and simple to use. For example, images must be placed in the correct place which is associates with the name of product. The name of product must be in bold writing along with the price so customers are attracted to the site.

Style

The style of the website must be formatted in a chronological order when it comes to browsing for products and filtering for option such as brands. The images and text must be aligned straight in an orderly manner so it’s simple and easy in the eye for customers.

Hosting

Choice of ISP

Choice of ISP is what provider is obtained to access the internet. There’s a variety of different service providers such as BT, sky, etc. The cheapest and largest amount of storage for data would need to be considered. The provider would need good upload speed and download for data transfer so users can still use the e-commerce site.

In house/Sub-contractor

Sub contractors are when the cost of the web hosting is more in comparison to in-house where it is cheaper. In house can be used when the business is small but when it expands a sub-contractor can be used.

Promotion

The Site

The E-commerce site can be advertised through the internet. Users who are already on the internet will already see the advertisement of the e-commerce which will make them go to the e-commerce site.

Search Engines

By inputting the e-commerce site into a search engine like google more users will access the site just by searching on the internet. So, by placing the e-commerce in a search engine, it will promote the site even more.

Costs

Setup

The company will need to pay for the hardware, software and networking. It can be brought or rented and you will require an experienced person to setup the system.

Maintenence

The market is expanding and the e-commerce site will need to regularly updated as products other parts of the business could be expired. This will also need experienced people to carry this out.

Security

The website and payment systems will need safe so customers don’t get put off which will attract less customers. Firewalls and anti-viruses and malware will need installing by experienced IT professionals.

Leasing

The cost of leasing will come from equipment and the website. The website can instead be leased or rented by the business monthly.

Advertising

Businesses will need to pay for advertisements on TV, radio and search engine listings.

Delivery strategy

Online shoppers will purchase their items online and then get there items delivered to them. Customers pay for delivery so, it won’t cost the business a lot however keep track of the status of the item is costly.

Staff Training

Businesses can carry host their own training which is cheaper if little training is required.

Security

Fraud Protection

The servers and websites will need to be secure and the website will also require a unique domain so customers are going to the correct site.

Hackers

Sometimes some users can change elements of the E-commerce webpage as if they are the web designer/developer. So, passwords should be updated and made much tougher so the webpage can verify the actual right user which enables extra protection in averse to the hacker.

Viruses

If there is a virus on an E-Commerce website then this will cause customers concerns which will lead to customers leaving the organization and then it would also lead to a loss of money. So, the administrator can install anti-virus software such as McAfee or Norton which would act as a protectoral barrier on an E-commerce website. By installing these Anti Viruses the potential threats can be found before they takeover and interfere with the entire system.
